Daftar Bab

Bab 1: Greetings

Greetings digunakan untuk menyapa:  
- Good morning  
- How are you?  
- Nice to meet you  

Digunakan dalam situasi formal dan informal.

Bab 2: Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta atau kebiasaan.  
Rumus: S + V1 (+s/es)  

Contoh:  
She goes to school every day.  
I eat rice every morning.

Bab 3: Pronouns

Kata ganti orang (pronouns):  
- I, you, he, she, it, we, they  
- me, him, her, us, them  

Contoh:  
He is my friend.  
They are watching TV.
